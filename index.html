About


The In4mer platform addresses the need for a business-networking platform, in contrast to social-networking platforms such as Whatsapp and Facebook.

It is fundamentally a messaging platform utilizing Apple's and Android's push notification services. The difference between the In4mer and other messenger apps is that the In4mer is context based.

THE APP


The In4mer is a highly customizable app which can fit your business's specific needs. The app is made of a variety of circles (mini apps) which can be taylored any use case, e.g. geo-tracking, QR code scanning, panic buttons and many more.
