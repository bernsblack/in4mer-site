About


The In4mer is a business networking platform which uses push notifications to notify users on business related topics.

The In4mer app comes with customizable mini apps which can be developed for your busnisses's needs. The app is used as a context based messaging platform where issues can be resolved in a quick and organised fasion.

THE APP


The In4mer is a highly customizable app which can fit your business's specific needs. The app is made of a variety of circles (mini apps) which can be taylored for any use case, e.g. geo-tracking, QR code scanning, panic buttons and many more.
